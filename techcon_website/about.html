Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and has grown into one of the largest technology conferences in the world. Each year, we bring together innovators, developers, and thought leaders to share ideas and shape the future of technology.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and education in the tech community. We aim to provide a platform for sharing knowledge, networking, and inspiring the next generation of technology leaders.

Notable Past Speakers

  • Elon Musk - CEO of SpaceX and Tesla. A visionary in space exploration and sustainable energy.

  • Sundar Pichai - CEO of Google. A leader in AI and cloud computing.

  • Satya Nadella - CEO of Microsoft. Driving digital transformation and innovation.

  • Sheryl Sandberg - COO of Meta. Advocate for women in tech and leadership.
